About the Photographer

Hi, I'm Vikram Palanisamy, the founder and creative force behind Agam Studio. Photography has been my passion since childhood. My journey began with a fascination for cameras, film rolls, and the art of developing photographs. Even at a young age, I spent countless hours learning how images were captured and brought to life through film processing.

With over 15 years of experience, I specialize in wedding photography, maternity photography, kids photography, event coverage, and candid storytelling. Every photograph is an opportunity to preserve genuine emotions and create memories that last a lifetime.

As a self-taught photographer and filmmaker, I have also worked on short film projects, which has helped shape my creative vision and storytelling approach. At Agam Studio, my goal is simple — to capture your most precious moments with authenticity, creativity, and attention to every detail.
